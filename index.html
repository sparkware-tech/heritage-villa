About Galle Heritage Villa

Heritage Villa
The Most Recommended Villa in Galle Fort

Located inside the iconic Galle Fort, Galle Heritage Villa blends colonial charm with modern comfort. Our spacious, air-conditioned rooms offer garden or courtyard views, soundproofing, free WiFi, and work desks for a restful stay.

Relax in the peaceful garden or on the terrace, just a 2-minute walk from Lighthouse Beach. With a prime location and warm hospitality, your time here promises to be both relaxing and memorable.
